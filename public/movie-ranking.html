The Best Movies of 2023 According to Ashrith

My top 3 movies are:

1.SALAAR

The comeback movie of our "darling Prabhas".
This movie is packed with full of Action,Emotion and Drama.

2.Animal

This is a film stuffed with some very good story with full of emotions
I have become a fan of Ranbir Kapoor after watching his performance in this film.
I have a special mention for the director Snadeep Reddy Vanga.

3.Kushi

Kushi staring my favourite "The Vijay Devarakonda" and "Samantha" is a beautiful Love Story.

4.Anni Manchi Sakunamule

Surprisingly this movie engaged me till the end with its Drama and Comedy.
I have enjoyed some healthy emotions between all the characters in this movie.

5.MAD

This movie is filled with out and out comedy drama with good friendship emotion.
The new cast of this movie did a grate job.
